Our Beginning

Locally rooted.
Globally minded.

Caribbean Homes was founded in 2008 in Bridgetown by a small team of native Barbadians who shared a conviction — that the Caribbean deserved a real estate agency as refined and particular as the islands themselves.

What began as a two-person office has grown into a regional boutique representing properties across 12 islands. Our agents live where they sell. They surf the reefs, know the restaurants, and have watched neighbourhoods evolve over decades.

That local intelligence — paired with a global standard of service — is why clients from London to Toronto to Singapore keep coming back.

Why Caribbean Homes

Local Expertise

Our agents aren't flown in — they live, work, and raise families on the islands they represent. They know which side of Mullins Bay the trade winds favour in December and which Soufrière streets flood after heavy rain.

That lived-in knowledge shapes every recommendation we make.

Curated Luxury

We decline more listings than we accept. Every property that carries the Caribbean Homes name has been personally visited, structurally reviewed, and judged against a consistent standard for location, craftsmanship, and investment integrity.

You will never waste a showing on something that shouldn't be on the market.

Full-Service Support

From the first virtual tour through legal closing and beyond, we coordinate every specialist you'll need — attorneys, surveyors, lenders, architects, property managers, relocation logistics.
