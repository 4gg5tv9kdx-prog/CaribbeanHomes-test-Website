About This Property

An Unrivalled Beachfront Experience

Perched along the platinum west coast of Barbados, this extraordinary beachfront villa represents the pinnacle of Caribbean luxury living. With direct access to the powder-white sands of Holetown Beach and 180-degree views of the Caribbean Sea, every sunrise and sunset is a private spectacle from the comfort of your own estate.

Spanning 4,200 square feet across a single expansive level, the villa's open-plan design masterfully blurs the boundary between indoor elegance and outdoor paradise. Floor-to-ceiling glass walls cascade light through the living spaces, while the retractable roof terrace and infinity-edge pool frame the ocean horizon in absolute perfection.

Five generously proportioned en-suite bedrooms — each appointed with custom millwork, imported Italian marble, and premium linens — offer sanctuary for both owners and guests. The chef's kitchen, equipped with Sub-Zero and Wolf appliances, completes this impeccably curated home.

Holetown is one of Barbados's most prestigious and sought-after addresses, situated along the famed Platinum Coast on the island's western shore. This historic town — the site of the first British settlement in 1627 — seamlessly blends colonial charm with contemporary luxury.

The neighbourhood of Sunset Crest offers an elevated residential enclave just minutes from the boutique restaurants, galleries, and designer shops of Holetown's First Street. Sandy Lane, one of the Caribbean's most celebrated resort hotels and golf courses, is a short 3-minute drive away. Bridgetown's international airport is approximately 20 minutes south.

The calm, reef-protected waters of the west coast make this stretch of coastline ideal for swimming, snorkelling, and water sports year-round. Neighbouring beaches including Paynes Bay and Mullins Beach are equally accessible, as are the marinas at Port St. Charles for sailing enthusiasts.
